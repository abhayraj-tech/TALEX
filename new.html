Frequently Asked Questions

What is TALEX? +

TALEX is a next-generation skill-based learning and hiring platform. It connects learning, earning, and hiring into one ecosystem — allowing you to build real-world skills, earn credits, collect verified badges, and get hired based on actual performance rather than traditional resumes.

How does the credit system work? +

You earn credits by completing courses, engaging with the community, and creating content. These credits can be used to unlock premium courses, access exclusive features, and boost your profile visibility to potential employers. It's a self-sustaining skill economy.

What are verified skill badges? +

Verified badges are proof-of-skill certifications backed by ratings, reviews, and project assessments. Unlike traditional certificates, badges on TALEX represent demonstrated ability — validated by both peers and industry experts. They ensure trust and transparency for employers.

How does AI-powered hiring work? +

Our AI hiring assistant analyzes candidates' verified badges, completed projects, skill ratings, and performance data to match them with the right opportunities. Companies get access to pre-vetted talent based on real skills — not just keywords on a resume.

Can I become a creator on TALEX? +

Absolutely! TALEX empowers students to become creators. Share your knowledge by building courses, earn credits for your content, and grow within the ecosystem. Apply through our creator program and start teaching what you know.
